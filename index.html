About
Sam Morgan (he/they) is a Mexican-Jamaican designer who brings brands to life through dynamic story driven animation and design.

Movement is at the core of everything I do—whether it’s in design, skateboarding, or cinematography. I bring a diverse perspective to my work, blending cultural influences with dynamic motion and storytelling to create high-impact visuals that help brands like Spotify and HP connect with their audiences in fresh, intentional, and captivating ways. From motion identities to social content and digital campaigns, I craft designs that resonate across digital and social platforms. At the heart of my design thinking is a passion for pushing ideas further. Whether refining a brand’s visual identity or skating through the city, I’m always seeking new ways to bring movement and meaning to the work I do. When I’m not designing, you’ll catch me skateboarding or taking neighborhood walks, drawing inspiration from everyday moments.
